Welcome to Laurent Callot's research page. I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the Vrije Universiteit (VU) Amsterdam within the department of Econometrics and OR. I am also affiliated with CREATES and the Tinbergen Institut.

My main research interests are in time series econometrics and high dimensional statistics, both theoretical and applied to macroeconomics and finance.
